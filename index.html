About Dr. Srivastava

Distinguished Neurosurgeon & Academic Leader

Dr. Arvind Kumar Srivastava is a highly accomplished neurosurgeon with over four decades of clinical excellence and academic contributions. Currently serving as Director-Professor and Consultant in the Department of Neurosurgery at G.B. Pant Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research, New Delhi, he leads Unit II and oversees the Neurosurgery ICU.

With extensive training in functional and stereotactic neurosurgery from Amsterdam Medical University, Dr. Srivastava has pioneered numerous neurosurgical techniques and contributed significantly to medical education in India. His expertise spans craniocerebral trauma, neuro-oncology, cerebrovascular diseases, and skull base surgery.

Special Recognition

Dr. Srivastava was personally deputed by the Government of India to provide neurosurgical care during the Hon'ble President's visit to Andaman & Nicobar Islands (1994) and was part of the first medical relief team to reach Bhuj during the Gujarat earthquake (2001).
